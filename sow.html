Section 1

Project Overview

Jarvis Daily ("Service Provider", a unit of Tranquil Veda Private Limited, GSTIN 27AAHCT6956C1Z3, SAC 998314) will deploy a private, production-ready instance of the Studio Command Centre for Aravali Studio Architects ("Client", Pune), configured around Aravali Studio's own templates, rate library, project schema and brand. Through this engagement Aravali Studio will:

  • Generate AI-drafted client proposals in Aravali Studio's firm voice, typically in under 10 minutes from a 2-minute brief
  • Edit any drafted proposal in natural language (English, Marathi or Hindi), with a visible diff per edit and rollback before send
  • Ship every proposal as a cinematic mini-website on Aravali Studio's subdomain with a one-click PDF export and an in-page "Call Anjali" CTA
  • Run the studio's daily standup on a WhatsApp team surface that replaces the verbal 2pm and 4pm rounds, with audit-trailed updates from designers, site-visit voice drops, and a 3D moodboard pinboard
  • Track BOQ versions, milestone payments, contractor outflows and project cashflow in a single screen per project and a rollup across the studio
  • Retain full ownership and export rights over every proposal, BOQ revision, client record and audit log; weekly Excel export to Google Drive is automatic
  • Maintain an immutable audit trail of every BOQ, proposal and payment change retained for 8 years, satisfying MCA Rule 3 of the Companies Amendment Rules 2022

The system is hosted on managed infrastructure procured and maintained by Jarvis Daily on Aravali Studio's behalf. Build duration is 7 calendar weeks from kickoff (4 build + 3 hypercare), followed by 30 days of daily-check-in hypercare and a 12-month support retainer commencing per Section 5.5.

5.5 Retainer Commencement

The monthly retainer commences on the earlier of (a) the calendar month in which go-live or final handover is achieved, or (b) the calendar month falling 10 weeks after the kickoff date. This dual-trigger is standard for managed-platform engagements and gives both sides a clear, calendar-anchored start to the steady-state support cycle. Where joint readiness checkpoints, configuration handovers, or sign-off cycles run past date (b), any continuing build work is carried out under the Change Request Process in Section 11 and date (b) remains the retainer-start date. Where the extension is attributable to Jarvis Daily or to Force Majeure under Section 11, date (b) moves on a day-for-day basis.

Section 7

Compliance Representation

Aravali Studio is responsible for the legality, taxation correctness and professional standards of every proposal sent through the system. Jarvis Daily is a software vendor and does not assume regulatory liability for the architectural advice contained within client proposals.

  • MCA Rule 3 audit trail. The Studio Command Centre maintains an immutable, append-only audit trail of every BOQ, proposal and payment record, retained for 8 years. The audit trail satisfies MCA Rule 3 of the Companies Amendment Rules 2022 for entities using accounting-software-adjacent systems.
  • GST and SAC/HSN. The Studio Command Centre's rate library supports GST tagging at the line-item level. SAC 998391 is applied to design services, SAC 9954 to site execution services, and HSN 9403 to furniture line items. Aravali Studio's CA remains the final authority on tax classification.
  • IT Act 2000. Digital signatures collected through the Client Sign-Off Portal are legally valid under Section 5 of the Information Technology Act 2000 and its 2008 amendment.
  • DPDP Act 2023. Aravali Studio's clients are treated as Data Principals. The system supports access, correction and erasure requests via the admin panel.

Section 11

Termination, Change Requests and Force Majeure

11.1 Termination for Convenience

Either party may terminate this SOW with thirty (30) days' written notice. On termination, Aravali Studio shall pay for all work completed up to the date of termination and Jarvis Daily shall deliver a final data export within 7 business days.

11.2 Change Request Process

Any out-of-scope work is documented in a written change request, sized in engineering hours by Jarvis Daily, and signed off by Aravali Studio's nominated authoriser before work begins. Change requests are billed at ₹3,000 per engineering hour + 18% GST.

11.3 Force Majeure

Neither party shall be liable for delays caused by acts of God, war, terrorism, civil unrest, government action, pandemic, or other events beyond reasonable control. Where Force Majeure extends the build, the retainer-commencement backstop date in Section 5.5 moves on a day-for-day basis.
